KOTOHIRA TRIP BASE -Kotori-

Kotori tourist information center operated by Kotobus

What is "Kotori"?

Customers using Kotobus Express can enjoy a cup of hot or ice coffee!

Please relax in the Kotori tourist information center operated by Kotobus.

"KOTOHIRA TRIP BASE -Kotori-" provides tourist information on Setouchi in Shikoku. It is 6-minute walk from the Kotohira-cho West Parking Lot, right in front of the Kotobus Express boarding point, Konpira Sando-guchi. We also provide on-site foreign language staff, kimono and bike rental services, workshops and organize other events.

Local taxi departure and arrival locations

  • Udon taxi
    A driver with specialized knowledge will guide you from the history and culture of udon to how to enjoy udon as well as udon ordering system.

  • Pilgrimage taxi
    A friendly driver will guide you. And you can also rent pilgrimage goods.
    (White coat, wagesa, and cane)

  • Sake taxi
    What about visitsing Kagawa's unique sake brewery, which is involved not only in making sake but also cosmetics and community development?

  • Konpira shuttle service
    We operate shuttle services taking you to the halfway to the Main shrine
Kotori service
  • Road bike rental

    Road bike rental service is available.
    Why don't you explore the city of Kotohira on a road bike?

  • Luggage storage

    Sightseeing with heavy luggage such as suitcases is difficult! Luggage storage service in Kotori is available.

  • Cafe corner

    Enjoy a cup of coffee (hot/ice) while using Kotobus!

  • Waiting area

    Cafe corner is available. You could meet your friends here too

  • Guided tour

    The unique walking tour guided by Kotori staff is highly recommended!
