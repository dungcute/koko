History

Kotohira Bus

September 1956

Established Kotohira Taxi at Kotohira 725-13 with the capital of 1.9 million yen

September 1961

Obtained license for general chartered passenger car transportation business
April 1968

Registered domestic travel

December 1969

Restructured to Kotohira Bus Co., Ltd.
Relocated the head office to 560-1 Enoi, Kotohira-cho
August 1976

Increased capital to 7 million yen
July 1977

Opened Takamatsu Office

February 1978

Increased capital to 25 million yen
February 1983

Increased capital to 40 million yen
February 1986

Increased capital to 60 million yen
November 1991

Established Sanuki Udon Factory

February 1998

Kagawa Prefecture, Travel Registration No. 2-194
July 2001

Established Udon no Yado Taishoya

September 2002

Re-elected and appointed Mr. Tetsuo Kusunoki as Representative Director
August 2003

Started operation of Udon Taxi (registered trademark)
August 2005

Started the operation of the express bus business "Kotobus Express"
October 2007

Relocated the head office to 1228-1 Kotohira-cho
May 2012

Established Tokushima Office in Naruto City, Tokushima Prefecture
July 2013

"Kotobus Express" is approved as an express bus company
August 2013

Re-elected and appointed Mr. Taijiro Kusunoki as Representative Director
