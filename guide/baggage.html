Luggage and Pets

Luggage and Pets

Please check the luggage policy as below:

Carry-on Luggage Size

  • Number of Carry-on luggage: 1 piece
  • The luggage must fit the overhead rack or space under your seat.
    (Please note that there is a heating system under your seat, then the area may be heating up)
  • If the luggage cannot be managed properly on the bus, our bus driver may ask you to keep your luggage in the bus trunk.

Carry-on Luggage Restrictions

Blade object

For safety reasons, we refuse to bring blades and other weapons that can be used as weapons into the car.
Hazardous material

Hazardous material and chemicals that cannot be brought into the bus nor kept in the trunk.
Explosives (including fireworks over 100g), gasoline, kerosene, alcohol, other flammable liquids, explosive substances, corrosive substances, etc.
Pets

Pets cannot be brought into the bus or be kept in the trunk.

Some items cannot be brought into the bus. Please keep it in the trunk.

Trunk luggage

・Big luggage such as a suitcase which cannot be kept on the bus should be stored in the trunk.
・We suggest you use your own landmarks, name tags, etc. to avoid mistake during taking your luggage.
・Each passenger is allowed to bring up to 2 pieces of trunk luggage.
・For security reasons, you can only receive your trunk luggage at the arrival place.

Maximum size for trunk luggage

For one piece of luggage: height + width + depth = 155cm maximum
For two pieces of luggage: height + width + dept = 240cm maximum

Trunk luggage restrictions

Fragile Items

Items that are easily breakable under vibration or susceptible to high temperatures are not allowed in the bus trunk.

Valuables

Please do not keep valuables in the trunk, including cash, precious metals, jewelry, securities, art antiques, or musical instruments.
Sporting Equipment

Skis, snowboards, and golf bags can be stored even if they exceed the specified size.
* For other sports equipment that exceeds the specified size, please contact our Customer Center after measuring the size (vertical, horizontal, height).
Bicycle

We will only accept a bicycle that is stored in a bicycle bag.
* A limited number of bicycles are allowed on the bus trunk. Please inform us in advance by contacting Kotobus Customer Center.
Attention

The operating bus company is not responsible for any loss, theft, damage, or other damage to the luggage.
