Change and cancellation

Instruction

How to change or cancel by phone

Cancellation by phone is available during business hours only. Please contact the Kotobus Customer Center and provide us with your ""reservation number"" and ""representative name"". Changing departure place: available until 15:00 on the departure date.

Kotobus Customer Center
10:00 - 18:00 every day
050-3537-5678

How to change or cancel from My Page

Log in to My Page and cancel the reservation before departure time. Please process: [Reservation Information]⇒ [Current Reservation]⇒ [Cancel].
* Please note that cancellation after departure time is not available. There is no refund for this case (cancellation fee is 100% of ticket price)

  1. 1. Select the bus you want to cancel

    From the current reservation list on My Page, proceed from the "Confirm/Change/Cancel" button for the bus you want to change.

  2. 2. Select Change or Cancel

    Please select "Change Reservation" or "Cancel Reservation" and proceed.

Refund

Refund method varies depending on the payment method

■ Payment at the convenience store: Refund by [Bank transfer] and [Kotobus point] are available.
■ Credit card payment: Refund to your credit card
■ Kotobus point: Refund by [Kotobus point]

Bank transfer

After changing or canceling your reservation, you will receive an e-mail "Refund process: Account registration request" the next business day. Please register your bank account information. We will refund to your registered account within 3 business days.
If you do not receive the "Refund process: Account registration request", or cannot register your bank account information, please contact the Kotobus Customer center and provide us with your reservation number.

Refund to credit cards

We will refund to your credit card company. Depending on the billing cycle of the credit card company, the refund procedure may take up to one or two months. Please check the statement from your card company.

Refund by Kotobus points (Online reservation only)

1 point is equivalent to 1 yen. Please find more information at:
https://www.kotobus-express.jp/member/

Cancellation fee

The cancellation fee will be charged if you cancel your booking. If your booking contains an accommodation plan, the cancellation fee will be different due to the change of the guest numbers in one room.
