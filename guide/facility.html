Facility

Free Wifi

You could access to free Wifi inside the bus for email checking, video watching on the Internet during the traveling time.
Please bring your own devices (personal computer, smartphone, tablet, game player etc.) that could connect to Wifi (wireless LAN) to enjoy.

Attention

  • Wifi could still be used after the light turns off, but please refrain from sound or light leakage that might disturb passengers around you.

Target routes and buses

  • Kagawa ・ Tokushima - Yokohama ・ Shinjuku ・ Tokyo ・ Tokyo Disneyland®
    for all scheduled Super Seat or Premium 3 buses [Bus number 701/702/703/704]
  • Kagawa ・ Tokushima - Yokkaichi ・ Nagoya
    for all scheduled Premium 3 buses
  • Tokushima ・ Kagawa - Hakata
    for all scheduled Premium 3 buses

※ Customers traveling from Ehime/Kochi can use Wi-Fi after transferring to the mainline.

Electric Outlet

"Premium 3 '' of Kotobus Express Tokyo is equipped with electric outlets

"Premium 3", three-seat-per-row high-speed buses of Kotobus Express Tokyo connecting Shikoku and Tokyo, are equipped with safe electrical outlets on every seat.
Moreover, you can use your smartphone or tablet with Wifi connection on the bus.
We are striving to make it more convenient for our customers when traveling on Kotobus Express Tokyo.

※ Please refrain from using any devices other than a mobile phone or tablet, as this may cause vehicle breakdown.
※Due to vehicle maintenance etc., we are sorry that there might be a substitute vehicle without electric outlets without notice prior to departure time.

Fukuoka - Nagoya route

There is no outlet available for each seat at the moment, but we have an outlet base for general use in the center of the bus.

※ The number of outlets is limited. (Up to 4)
※ This is a shared space. Please be careful with your own properties and personal information.

Toilet

Premium 3 and Super Seat buses are equipped with toilets.

Buses will stop at parking areas (PA) and service areas (SA) during the break. (Customers cannot get on and off at SA/PA when the bus stops for driver change) We will refrain from announcements of the stop inside the bus and only use spare lights in the vehicle. (Departure time will be posted near the entrance.) Rest areas are changeable depending on road conditions.

Blanket and others

We will provide you with a blanket that can cover almost your whole body. Even if you fold it in half, you can cover it from the chest to around the knee, so you can easily use it to maintain your body temperature.

We also have masks and hangers in our buses.
Feel free to contact the driver if necessary.
(Some vehicles are excluded)

Drinking corner

You can enjoy the free drink in the bar on Premium 3, Super Seat buses.
※ Hot drink only
※ Cannot be used during the drive.
※ Some vehicles are excluded.
