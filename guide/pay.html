Payment method

Available payment method: Credit Card Payment/Convenience Store Payment/Internet Banking

Payment at Convenience Store

You can pay by cash at Lawson, Family Mart, and Seiko Mart (Hokkaido and Kanto areas). In addition, there is a payment deadline according to the reservation date. Please note that if there is no payment by the payment deadline, the reservation will be automatically canceled.

The reservation date is more than 8 days before departure:

The payment deadline is until 23:59 of the third day from the reservation date

The reservation date is 2 to 7 days before departure:

The payment deadline is until 23:59 of the following day after the reservation date

The reservation date is 1 day before or on the departure date:

The payment deadline is until the departure time However, if the departure is after 24:00, the payment deadline will be until 23:59 of the day before the arrival date.
