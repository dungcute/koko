How to book

Online reservation by PC or smartphone

You can check the ticket availability and make a new reservation with your PCs and smartphones by following the instruction below:
※The following screen is on PC.

  1. 1. Search routes and select the bus

    Please check the routes and select the bus by inputting boarding place, departure time, arrival time, seat type, etc.,

  2. Select your boarding place and number of passengers

    Please select your desired boarding area and enter the number of passengers.

  3. 3. Select Plan/Seat Type

    Please select your desired plan and seat type

  4. 4. Check the reservation details then add to the shopping cart

    Please check the bus number, boarding place, arriving place, the number of people, plan, seat type, price, add it to the shopping cart to proceed with the reservation process.

  5. 5. Login/New member registration

    Membership registration is required for booking. If you are already a member, please enter your email address (login ID) and password to log in. If you are not a member, please register as a new member first.

  6. 6. Passenger information

    Enter the passenger information (name, e-mail address, passenger name, etc.) and select the payment method.

  7. 7. Payment methods

    Please choose "credit card payment" or "convenience store payment/Others".

  8. 8. Complete

    Please check the "Reservation number" and your mailbox for the reservation confirmation email. Please confirm the reservation number as you will need it when boarding. (※1)

(※1) Reservation confirmation email: You may not receive this email because of your email setting. Please cancel the rejection setting and register the domain of the sender address "kotobus-express.jp"

Booking by phone

Please contact Kotobus Customer Center if you have any inquiries regarding ticket availability, new reservations, changes, cancellations, and lost items.

Kotobus customer center
10:00 - 18:00 every day
050-3537-5678
  • There is no need to visit a ticket counter! You can buy the ticket online and receive the reservation confirmation email with a booking number to board our bus.
  • Please check the information on our website or pamphlet, etc., and contact us for ticket availability inquiries or reservations.
  • Booking by phone: Available payment methods are bank transfer, credit card, and convenience store payment.
  • Lost and found: Please contact our Customer center. Please note that it takes time to confirm the lost item's status.
  • Reservation through agents or other websites: We are very sorry that the cancellation request cannot be processed. Please contact the agents or websites you made the reservation directly for better support.
