About Kotobus

Kotobus Express is a highway express bus that connects Kagawa / Tokushima / Kochi / Ehime with Tokyo / Nagoya.
Your safety is our priority, then we always make various efforts to provide comfortable buses.

Kotobus is the first company in Shikoku acquired the international standard “ISO39001 (Road Traffic Safety)” certification. In addition to management during operation, the alcohol checker, digital tachograph, and drive recorder are used before and after the operation to ensure physical conditions and safety guidance for each driver. All the vehicles are equipped with the latest safety devices such as PCS (Pre-Collision System), vehicle wanders alarm, and VSC (vehicle stability control) to prevent traffic accidents.

In order to provide comfortable bus trips for customers, we actively incorporating customer feedback. For example, items frequently requested by customers, such as masks, hangers, and blankets, are available for all vehicles. Please feel free to contact the bus driver when you need it. To avoid mistake during taking your luggage, please use your own name tags. You can also check the ticket availability and make the reservation online anytime on your PC and mobile phone.

The vehicles with independent three-row seats that allow you to relax in a comfortable space. The outlets is also available to charge smartphones and laptops.

Kotobus is a reasonable, safe and comfortable way to reach your destination. Please enjoy our trip from Kagawa / Tokushima / Kochi / Ehime to the Tokyo / Nagoya / Fukuoka area with us.
