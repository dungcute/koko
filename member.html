Guidance for membership

Kotobus point

If you make an online reservation for express buses or tour products at Kotobus Express, you will get 1 Kotobus point for every 100 yen. Kotobus point could be used for payment of online reservations for express bus tours and products at the Kotobus Express site (1 Kotobus point corresponds to 1 yen).

About point grant

Points will be granted 3 days after the last traveling date.

Check Point Usage/Accumulation History

You can check the history of points used and earned in" My Page Top> Point History/Balance".
※ Point history will be displayed within the past year.

Details about Kotobus point

  • Points could only be earned and used for online reservations (PC/mobile). Reservations by telephone are not applicable.
  • Points are usually valid for 3 years from the date of the grant. However, the expiration date of each type of point varies depending on each campaign.
  • When you use points, they will be automatically deducted from the points closest to their expiry date.
  • Please note that even if you use points before the expiration date, if you make any cancellation or changes after the expiration date, those points will not be refunded.
  • If you cancel or change after booking, you will earn points for the reduced price.

※Kotobus point is a discount service provided by Kotohira Bus Co., Ltd.
