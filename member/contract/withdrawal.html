Terms and Conditions of Kotobus Membership Withdrawal

Be sure to read this before canceling your Kotobus Membership (henceforth referred to as "Membership").

  1. These terms, as set forth by Kotohira Bus Co., Ltd. (henceforth referred to as "the Company"), define the conditions of cancellation of Membership by a Kotobus Member (henceforth referred to as "the Member"). In the case of the Member holding previously made travel reservations, the Member cannot cancel Membership before the departure date of said reservations. The cancellation process will begin after all travel reservations have been completed.
  2. The Member, upon canceling Membership, shall be considered as having understood the terms below.
    • From the day of cancellation onward, any member-based services offered by any websites operated by the Company will no longer be available for use.
    • Any Kotobus Points remaining as of the time of cancellation will be rendered invalid.
    • Even after cancellation, the Member must take responsibility for payment of any and all fees incurred to the Member's credit card, etc.
  3. Once the membership termination procedure is accepted, we will delete the member information as described below.
    • Member information such as registration information, mail data, profile, etc. will be deleted
    • Personal information, acquisition points, profile contents, e-mail contents, etc. cannot be recovered under any circumstances.
