JR Utazu Station South Exit

Reception
Bus station
Operation company
Kotohira Bus Co., Ltd.

Remarks

About 3 minutes on foot from the south exit of JR Utazu Station. Go out the south exit of JR Utazu Station and proceed to the roundabout on your left.
The bus stop is located on the opposite side of a restaurant, namely 讃岐の味 塩がま屋 (Sanuki no Mami Shiogamaya Utazu Branch).
There is a parking lot nearby (500 JPY for 24 hours).
