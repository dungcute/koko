Kotobus Station Kotohira

Reception
Bus station
Operation company
Kotohira Bus Co., Ltd.

Remarks

About 10 minutes by car from JR Kotohira Station. Follow National Highway No. 377 toward Yamamoto town. The bus stop is in an open space on the left, passing the traffic light at Ikoinosato Entrance, which leads to Vispo Kotohira. [Former: Udon Stop]
