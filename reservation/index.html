How to book a bus ticket

Online reservation

You can check the ticket availability and make a new reservation with your PCs and smartphones by following the instruction below:

  1. 1. Search routes and select the bus

    Please check the routes and select the bus by inputting boarding place, departure time, arrival time, seat type, etc.,

  2. 2. Select the boarding place and number of passengers

    Please select your desired boarding area and enter the number of passengers.

  3. 3. Select Plan/Seat Type

    Please select your desired plan and seat type

  4. 4. Check the bus details to add to cart

    Please check the bus number, boarding place, arrival place, number of passengers, plan, seat type, price, add it to the shopping cart to proceed with the reservation.

  5. 5. Login/New member registration

    Membership registration is required for reservation. If you are already a member, please enter your email address (login ID) and password to log in.
    If you are not a member, please register as a new member first.

  6. 6. Passenger information

    Enter the passenger information (name, e-mail address, passenger name, etc.) and select the payment method.

  7. 7. Payment method

    Please choose "credit card payment" or "convenience store payment/other".

  8. 8. Complete the reservations

    Please check the "Reservation number" and your email address. Please confirm the reservation number as you will need it when boarding.

Booking by phone

Please contact Kotobus Customer Center if you have any inquiries regarding ticket availability, new reservations, changes, cancellations, and lost items.

  • There is no need to visit a ticket counter! You can buy the ticket online and receive the reservation confirmation email with a booking number to board our bus.
  • Please check the information on our website or pamphlet, etc., and contact us for information on ticket availability or reservation.
  • Reservation by phone: Available payment methods are bank transfer, credit card, and convenience store payment.
  • Lost and found: Please contact our Customer center. Please note that it takes time to confirm the lost item status.
  • Reservation through agents or another website: We are very sorry that no change/cancellation can be completed. Please contact the agents or website you made the reservation directly for better support.
