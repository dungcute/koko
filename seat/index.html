Premium 3

  • Premium (bound for Tokyo)
    3 seats in a row, equipped with toilet and electrical outlets(However, occasionally, due to vehicle inspection/maintenance, an alternative bus will be arranged without notice).
  • 3 separated seats in a row!
    Footrests, leg rests, and detachable headrests are well equipped.
  • Relaxing time
    Our buses will take short breaks along the ride. You can utilize it for refreshing or toilets. Also, there is a clean toilet inside the bus.
