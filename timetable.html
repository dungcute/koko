Kotobus tickets will be released 03 months in advance

Kotobus will be released at 10:00 AM, 03 months in advance prior to your departure date.

Kotobus is licensed to operate on highway routes by the Ministry of Transportation. Previously known as Shikoku bus company, Kotobus is the first company in Shikoku acquired the international standard “ISO39001 (Road Traffic Safety)” certification, and established a 24-hour operation management system. In addition to management during operation, the alcohol checker, digital tachograph, and drive recorder are used before and after the operation to ensure physical conditions and safety guidance for each driver.
