Icon descriptions

● Icons used for seats

Single private seat
The space between this seat and the next nearest one makes it possible to sleep or move about without disturbing anyone else.		 フットレスト
足の疲れを軽減する足置き（フットレスト）付のシートです。
2 seats in a row
There are two seats per row. Wide seats and private room type seats are available.		 レッグレスト
足のふくらはぎ部分を休められるレッグレスト付のシートです。
3 seats in a row
There are three seats per row. Wide seats and individual seats are both available.		 Blanket
This seat comes with a blanket for use during travel.
4 seats in a row
This is the standard seat arrangement with a total of four rows, two on each side of the aisle.		 With restroom
The bus is equipped with a restroom.
Ladies seat policy
Female passengers (who are traveling alone or in a group of an odd number) are always seated next to other female passengers to ensure safe and comfortable overnight travel.		 Privacy curtains
Curtains separate the neighboring seat, giving the feel of an individual compartment.
